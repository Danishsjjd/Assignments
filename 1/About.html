Saylani Welfare International Trust

Saylani Welfare International Trust is a non-government organizaion focusing primarily on feeding the poor and homeless.It was established in May 1999 and is headquartered at Bahduabad,Karachi,Pakistan.It was founded and headed by spiritual and religious scholar Maulana Bashir Farooq Qadri.
